An exclusive horse riding center for you!
Our mission is to provide riders and horse lovers a unique place to train and enjoy the riding experience.

We are well equipped to cater for every level of ability from the total beginner to the exam candidate wanting a career with horses in an environment that is fun, friendly and progressive.

At Spirit you will find a professional and cheerful staff who will be able to lead an excellent training for horse and rider. Our priority is the welfare and safety of horses and riders.
